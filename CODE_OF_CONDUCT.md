# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The Gender Equity Intelligence Platform is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to:
- Creating an **inclusive and welcoming environment** for all contributors
- Treating all individuals with **respect and dignity**
- Supporting **gender equality and social justice**
- Building technology that serves **marginalized communities**
- Maintaining the **highest ethical standards**

## Our Values

1. **Respect**: We value diverse perspectives and experiences
2. **Inclusion**: Everyone's voice matters
3. **Integrity**: We act ethically and transparently
4. **Collaboration**: We work together toward common goals
5. **Impact**: We focus on advancing gender equality
6. **Accountability**: We take responsibility for our actions

## Expected Behavior

Participants in this community are expected to:

### Show Respect
- Use inclusive language that doesn't stereotype or discriminate
- Respect pronouns and chosen names
- Listen actively and acknowledge different viewpoints
- Value contributions regardless of experience level
- Be patient and supportive with new members

### Maintain Professionalism
- Communicate clearly and respectfully
- Focus on issues, not personal attacks
- Assume good intent in others' words and actions
- Keep discussions on topic and productive
- Be punctual and reliable

### Foster Inclusivity
- Welcome people from all backgrounds
- Actively work to include underrepresented voices
- Use trigger warnings when appropriate
- Provide alternative formats for accessibility
- Be aware of cultural differences

### Support Community Goals
- Help newer members learn and grow
- Share knowledge and expertise generously
- Contribute constructively to discussions
- Credit others' work and ideas
- Advocate for gender equity values

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are NOT tolerated:

### Harassment & Discrimination
- Verbal abuse, insults, or derogatory comments
- Discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Sexual harassment or unwanted sexual advances
- Stalking or threatening behavior
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Deliberate misgendering or inappropriate deadnaming

### Exclusionary Conduct
- Gatekeeping or dismissive attitudes toward newcomers
- Deliberately excluding people from conversations
- Interrupting or talking over others repeatedly
- Aggressive or condescending tone
- Dismissing concerns about bias or discrimination

### Disruptive Behavior
- Spam or repetitive off-topic messages
- Trolling or intentionally provocative behavior
- Spreading misinformation
- Disrupting project work or governance
- Using the platform to promote unrelated products/services

### Professional Misconduct
- Breaching confidentiality or privacy
- Misrepresenting credentials or contributions
- Plagiarism or intellectual property theft
- Using community data unethically
- Conflicts of interest without disclosure

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document the Incident**
   - Screenshot or copy relevant messages
   - Note date, time, and context
   - Identify any witnesses

2. **Report It**
   - Email: [conduct@geipshield.org](mailto:conduct@geipshield.org)
   - For urgent issues or safety concerns: [safety@geipshield.org](mailto:safety@geipshield.org)
   - Reports are confidential and taken seriously

3. **What to Include**
   - Description of the incident
   - Who was involved
   - When and where it occurred
   - How this impacted you or others
   - Any supporting evidence

### Response Process

1. **Initial Review** (72 hours)
   - Acknowledgment of report
   - Initial assessment of severity

2. **Investigation** (7-14 days)
   - Interviews with involved parties
   - Collection of evidence
   - Assessment against this Code of Conduct

3. **Resolution** (varies)
   - Warnings or corrections
   - Temporary suspension
   - Permanent removal (in severe cases)
   - Follow-up support for affected individuals

4. **Appeals**
   - Written appeals within 14 days
   - Review by independent committee
   - Final decision within 7 days

## Consequences

Violations may result in:

### First Offense
- **Verbal Warning**: Private communication about behavior
- **Written Warning**: Formal notice with remediation expectations
- **Temporary Suspension**: 7-30 day ban from contributions/discussions

### Repeated Offenses
- **Extended Suspension**: 30-90 day ban
- **Permanent Ban**: Removal from community

### Severe Violations
- **Immediate Ban**: Dangerous or illegal behavior
- **Legal Action**: If required by law
- **Report to Authorities**: For safety threats or crimes
